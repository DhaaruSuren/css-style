Rose

The rose is a type of flowering shrub. Its name comes from the Latin word Rosa. The flowers of the rose grow in many different colors, to from the well-known red rose or yellow roses and sometimes white or purple roses. Most roses have prickles (incorrectly called thorns) on their stems
